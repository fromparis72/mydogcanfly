## Our mission

**My Dog Can Fly** was born from a simple conviction: *the world is more beautiful when shared with your dog*. Too many dog owners still give up on trips, adventures or even a summer getaway because they're afraid of getting it wrong. Our goal is to remove those barriers with information that is **reliable, practical and fact-checked** — so that every trip, from a weekend close to home to a flight across the ocean, goes smoothly for you and your companion.

## What you'll find here

We publish practical guides organized around four themes:

- **Traveling with your dog** — by car, train or plane, plus paperwork and international travel.
- **Dog-friendly stays & destinations** — where to go, where to sleep, and how to choose well.
- **Health & well-being on the road and through the seasons** — heatstroke, paw pads, motion sickness, first aid.
- **Gear & practical advice** — choosing the right travel equipment for your dog.

## Our method

Every article is grounded in **official and veterinary sources** (veterinary bodies, government agencies, airlines and other carriers), cited at the bottom of each page. Our content is **dated and updated** regularly to stay accurate.

> **Disclaimer.** Our health articles are for information only and **never replace the advice of a veterinarian**. If you have any doubt about your dog's condition, consult a professional.
