## Data we collect

This site is a static blog. We do not collect any personal data without your knowledge. Data may be processed only if:

- you email us (your email address and the content of your message);
- you use a contact form, where one is provided;
- privacy-friendly audience measurement tools are enabled.

## Cookies and analytics

We favor **privacy-friendly analytics** (for example Cloudflare Web Analytics or Plausible), **with no tracking cookies** and no collection of personally identifiable data. If third-party cookies were ever added, a consent banner would be shown.

In accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation and similar privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct and delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, write to [fromparis+mydogcanfly@gmail.com](mailto:fromparis+mydogcanfly@gmail.com).
