## How to choose the right tool for your trip

Each tool covers one specific step of planning a trip with your dog. The **flight comparison tool** helps you find and choose the airline that best fits your route and your dog's weight — in the cabin, in the hold or as cargo. The **IATA crate calculator** works out the size of airline-approved crate your dog needs if they travel in the hold. And the **entry requirements checker** lists, country by country, the documents and steps you must complete before departure. The **heat checker** tells you, from your location, whether it's too hot to walk your dog or fly them in the hold today.

Used together, these four tools cover the essentials: which airline, which crate, which paperwork — and whether the heat is safe. Everything you need to travel with your dog with peace of mind.

## Traveling with your dog: the key steps

1. **Check your destination's entry requirements** (microchip, vaccination and rabies titer test, passport, health certificate) with the [entry requirements checker](/tools/dog-entry-requirements-by-country/).
2. **Choose the airline** that accepts your dog on your route with the [dog flight comparison tool](/tools/can-my-dog-fly/) and our [comparison of 62 airline pet policies](/airline-pet-policies/).
3. **Work out the crate size** with the [IATA crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/), then get an [airline-approved travel crate](/airline-approved-dog-crate/).
4. **Prepare your dog** for the flight to keep stress down: see our advice on [dog travel anxiety](/dog-travel-anxiety/).

## Frequently asked questions

**Are these tools free?** Yes, all three tools are completely free, with no sign-up required. They are provided for guidance only: always confirm the details with the airline and the official authorities before booking.

**Can my dog fly?** In most cases, yes. Based on your dog's weight and your route, the comparison tool shows you which airlines accept them in the cabin or in the hold.

**What crate size should I choose?** It depends on your dog's measurements, not just their weight: the IATA crate calculator gives you the exact size.

More tools are in the works, including a trip cost estimator for traveling with your dog.
