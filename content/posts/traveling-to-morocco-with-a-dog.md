## Entry requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| 💉 Microchip | Identification is mandatory. |
| 🦠 Valid rabies vaccination | Must be up to date. |
| 📄 Official health certificate | Issued by an official veterinarian within 24 hours before boarding. |
| 🩺 Certificate of good health | Veterinary exam at least 10 days before arrival. |

{{< alerte type="verifier" >}}
If you're returning to the EU from Morocco, a rabies titer test (serology, ≥ 0.5 IU/ml, drawn at least 30 days after vaccination) is required — plan for it before you leave. If you live elsewhere, check your own country's re-entry rules well in advance.

## Practical tips

Plan ahead: most refusals come down to a missing document or a missed deadline (vaccination too recent, rabies titer test, tapeworm treatment outside its window). Put together a folder with every supporting document, and keep a copy. Also get your dog ready for the journey — see our advice on [travel anxiety in dogs](/dog-travel-anxiety/) and the [dog first aid kit](/dog-first-aid-kit/).
