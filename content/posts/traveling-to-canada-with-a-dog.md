## Entry requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| 🦠 Rabies vaccination certificate | In French or English, stating the microchip number, vaccination date, expiration date and vaccine brand. |
| 🗓️ Over 3 months old | Rabies vaccination is mandatory for dogs over 3 months old. |
| 💉 Microchip | The chip number must appear on the certificate. |

## How to get there with your dog

You can travel [by plane](/airline-pet-policies/) (compare the airlines that accept dogs). Plan the transport with our [dog flight checker](/tools/can-my-dog-fly/) and, for the hold, size the crate with the [IATA dog crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/).

## Practical tips

Plan ahead: most refusals come down to a missing document or a missed deadline (vaccine given too recently, rabies titer test, tapeworm treatment outside the required window). Put together a file with all the supporting documents, and keep a copy. Also prepare your dog for the journey — see our advice on [travel anxiety](/dog-travel-anxiety/) and the [dog first aid kit](/dog-first-aid-kit/).
