Hoping to fly with your dog on Wizz Air? It is one of the few airlines that does **not accept pets** on board, either in the cabin or in the hold — only recognized service dogs are admitted. Here's what that means, and above all the **alternatives** to reach your destination with your dog.

## In the cabin: no

Wizz Air does not allow dogs in the cabin — service dogs only.

## In the hold: no

Wizz Air does not offer hold transport for dogs. Service dogs only.

## How to book

Pet spots on board are limited: **book as early as possible**.

1. Buy your ticket, then **contact Wizz Air** to add your dog to the booking.
2. Prepare the **health documents** (microchip ID, valid rabies vaccination, pet passport or health certificate depending on your departure country, sometimes a rabies titer test) — see our guide to [international travel with a dog](/international-travel-with-a-dog/).
3. Choose the right container: an approved soft carrier (cabin) or an [IATA crate](/airline-approved-dog-crate/) (hold). Size it with our [IATA dog crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/).
