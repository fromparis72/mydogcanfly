On the road, a minimum of grooming keeps your dog clean, comfortable, and welcome at your accommodations. A small dedicated kit keeps you from forgetting the essentials — and from dirtying the car or the rental.

## The express cleanup

A **microfiber towel** quickly dries a wet dog (rain, swim, mud) before getting into the car or entering the rental. **Alcohol-free wipes** come in handy for paws and around the eyes. After the ocean, a **fresh-water rinse** prevents the itching caused by salt.

## Brushing and nails

Pack a **brush** suited to your dog's coat (especially for long stays and long coats, which snag foxtails). For an extended trip, **nail clippers** or a file keep the nails in check. A **dry shampoo** tides you over between real baths.

## Cleanup and good manners

Always keep **waste bags** within reach: it's basic courtesy and often a legal requirement. Bring something to clean up a small accident in a rental (paper towels, a suitable cleaner).
