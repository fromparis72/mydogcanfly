Avianca (Colombia) is one of the carriers that accepts dogs **both in the cabin and in the hold**, under certain conditions. Weight limits, fees, breed restrictions and booking: here's everything you need to know before you reserve.

## In the cabin

Avianca accepts a dog in the cabin if its weight, carrier included, does not exceed **10 kg (22 lb)**. The carrier must be soft-sided and fit under the seat in front of you. Expect around **€41–202 (~$45–230)** depending on the route.

*Airline conditions:* ≤10 kg (22 lb) with carrier.

## In the hold

Above the cabin limit, your dog travels in the pressurized, heated hold in an [IATA-compliant crate](/airline-approved-dog-crate/), with a combined dog + crate weight of up to **70 kg (154 lb)**. Indicative fee: **€64–267 (~$75–305)**.

*Airline conditions:* ≤70 kg (154 lb); brachycephalic breeds banned from the hold.

## Snub-nosed (brachycephalic) breeds

Like most airlines, Avianca restricts **flat-faced breeds** (Bulldogs, Pugs, Boxers, etc.), which are generally **banned from the hold** because of the respiratory risk. If your dog is brachycephalic, opt for the cabin whenever their weight allows it, and confirm the rule before booking.

> ⚠️ **Heat:** **seasonal embargoes** may apply during periods of high temperatures. Check the dates of your trip. Our advice: [heatstroke in dogs](/dog-heatstroke/).

## How to book

Pet spots on board are limited: **book as early as possible**.

1. Buy your ticket, then **contact Avianca** to add your dog to the reservation.
2. Prepare the **health documents** (microchip ID, rabies vaccination, health certificate or pet passport, sometimes a rabies titer test) — see our guide to [international travel with a dog](/international-travel-with-a-dog/).
3. Choose the right container: an approved soft-sided carrier (cabin) or an [IATA crate](/airline-approved-dog-crate/) (hold). Size it with our [IATA dog crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/).
