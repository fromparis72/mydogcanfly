Volotea (Spain) carries dogs **in the cabin only**: convenient for small dogs, but a non-starter if yours is too heavy. Here are the exact conditions, the fee and the steps to follow.

## In the cabin

Volotea accepts a dog in the cabin if its weight, carrier included, does not exceed **10 kg (22 lb)**. The carrier must be soft-sided and fit under the seat in front of you. Expect about **€39–60 (~$45–70)**.

*Airline conditions:* 10 kg (22 lb) including accessories; soft carrier max 50 × 40 × 20 cm (20 × 16 × 8 in); not available to/from the UK, Ireland or Malta.

## In the hold: no

Volotea does not offer hold transport for dogs. No pets in the hold.

## How to book

Pet spots on board are limited: **book as early as possible**.

1. Buy your ticket, then **contact Volotea** to add your dog to the booking.
2. Prepare the **health documents** (microchip ID, valid rabies vaccination, pet passport or health certificate depending on your departure country, sometimes a rabies titer test) — see our guide to [international travel with a dog](/international-travel-with-a-dog/).
3. Choose the right container: an approved soft carrier (cabin) or an [IATA crate](/airline-approved-dog-crate/) (hold). Size it with our [IATA dog crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/).
