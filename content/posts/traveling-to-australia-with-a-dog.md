## Entry requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| 📝 Import permit (DAFF) | Once all rabies-related conditions are met (about 6–7 months ahead). |
| 💉 Microchip | Linked to the permit and to all documents. |
| 🧪 Rabies titer test (RNATT) | ≥ 0.5 IU/ml, blood drawn ≥ 180 days before entry (valid 12 months). |
| 🏥 Mandatory quarantine | Minimum 10 days at the Mickleham facility (Melbourne). |

{{< alerte type="check" >}}
Every procedure must be performed by an approved veterinarian. Check which DAFF country group your country of departure belongs to.

## How to get there with your dog

You can travel [by plane](/airline-pet-policies/) (compare airlines that accept dogs). Plan the transport with our [dog flight checker](/tools/can-my-dog-fly/) and, for the hold, size the crate with the [IATA dog crate calculator](/tools/iata-dog-crate-calculator/).

## Practical tips

Plan ahead: most refusals come down to a missing document or a missed deadline (a vaccine given too recently, the rabies titer test, a tapeworm treatment outside its window). Put together a file with every supporting document, and keep a copy. Also get your dog ready for the trip — see our advice on [travel anxiety](/dog-travel-anxiety/) and the [dog first aid kit](/dog-first-aid-kit/).
