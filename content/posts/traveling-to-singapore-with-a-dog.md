{{< enbref >}}
- 🎯 Difficulty level: **Very demanding**
- 📝 **Import license (AVS/NParks)** required
- 💉 **Microchip** + **rabies vaccine**
- 🧪 **Rabies titer test** (≥ 28 days after the vaccine)
- 🏥 Possible **quarantine** (~30 days) depending on the category
- ⏳ Timeline: **several months**
{{< /enbref >}}

Singapore applies a **strict, lengthy** import procedure. The quarantine length and conditions depend on the rabies risk category Singapore assigns to your country of origin: it is essential to check with the Singaporean authorities (NParks/AVS) well in advance.

## Entry requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| 📝 Import license (AVS/NParks) | Mandatory; conditions depend on the rabies risk category of the country of origin. |
| 💉 Microchip + rabies vaccine | Identification and vaccination up to date. |
| 🧪 Rabies titer test (serological) | Blood sample drawn ≥ 28 days after the vaccine (≥ 0.5 IU/ml). |
| 🏥 Possible quarantine | Up to ~30 days in an approved facility, depending on the assigned category. |

{{< alerte type="warning" >}}
The process takes **several months** (license + rabies titer test + possible quarantine). Plan very far ahead and confirm every step with **NParks/AVS**.
