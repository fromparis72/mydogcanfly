Walks at dawn, at dusk, or after dark — frequent when traveling, especially at highway rest stops — put your dog at risk if drivers and cyclists can't see them. A few light-up accessories change everything.

## Being seen from afar

A rechargeable **LED collar or harness** makes your dog visible from dozens of meters (over 100 ft) away, head-on and from the side. It's especially useful for dark-coated dogs, whether in the city or at a poorly lit rest stop. Check the model's **battery life** and **water resistance**.

## Reflective gear, as a complement

**Reflective strips** on the harness and leash bounce back headlight beams with no battery needed. Combined with an LED light source, they provide double security. A reflective leash also protects the owner, who is often standing at the roadside too.

## Identification, always up to date

Visibility doesn't replace **identification**: keep a legible tag with an **up-to-date phone number**, in addition to the microchip. When you travel, a dog that slips away at night needs to find their way back to you fast.
