A Spanish low-cost carrier in the IAG group (alongside Iberia and British Airways), Vueling flies a dense network across Spain, France and the Mediterranean, with a hub in **Barcelona**. For owners of small and medium dogs, it has one precious advantage: a **more generous cabin weight limit** than most airlines.

## In the cabin: up to 10 kg (22 lb), a real advantage

Where Air France, KLM or Lufthansa stop at 8 kg (18 lb), **Vueling allows up to 10 kg (22 lb)** (dog + carrier). That 2 kg (4.4 lb) difference changes everything for a dachshund, a light cocker spaniel or a small mixed breed: it can travel **in the cabin**, next to you, rather than in the hold.

The carrier must be soft-sided and measure at most **45 × 39 × 21 cm (18 × 15 × 8 in)**. The fee is among the lowest on the market: expect about **€30–60 (~$35–70)** depending on the flight. Book early, because the number of pets per flight is limited.

## No hold

Vueling **accepts no dogs in the hold**. If your dog weighs more than 10 kg (22 lb), this airline is not an option: you'll need a carrier that offers the hold, such as [Iberia](/iberia-dog-policy/) (same group), [Air France](/air-france-dog-policy/) or [Transavia](/transavia-dog-policy/).

## Destination restrictions

An important point that's often overlooked: Vueling **does not carry dogs to the United Kingdom, Ireland or Iceland**, because of the specific animal-health regulations of these island nations. Check your destination before booking. For other countries' entry rules, see our guide to [international travel with a dog](/international-travel-with-a-dog/).

## How to book

1. When booking your flight, **add the "pet in cabin" option**. The number of pet spots is limited.
2. Check that your destination **is not excluded** (UK, Ireland, Iceland).
3. Prepare the **documents** (microchip, valid rabies vaccination, pet passport or health certificate depending on your departure country) and a **compliant soft carrier**.

Since Vueling serves many summer and beach destinations, think about your dog's comfort in hot weather: see our advice on [heatstroke](/dog-heatstroke/) and our ideas for [dog-friendly beaches](/dog-friendly-beaches/).

## If Vueling isn't the right fit

Dog over 10 kg (22 lb), or headed to the UK? Look at [Iberia](/iberia-dog-policy/) (hold up to 45 kg / 99 lb) or the full-service carriers. Our [airline pet policy comparison](/airline-pet-policies/) helps you find the right alternative for your route.
