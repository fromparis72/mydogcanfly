Our credibility rests on a clear method. Here is what we commit to.

## Accuracy and sources

Every article is built on **primary, authoritative sources**: veterinary publications, government agencies, airlines and other carriers, and official regulators. These sources are **cited at the bottom of each article**.

## Expertise and experience (E-E-A-T)

Our content is written and signed by an identified author, whose [profile](/author/) you can read. We prioritize **real-world, first-hand experience**, backed by verifiable references.

## Keeping content current

Every article displays its **publication date and last-updated date**. We periodically review our guides to reflect regulatory changes (transportation, international travel) and evolving best practices.

## Independence

Our recommendations are **editorial and independent**. When we mention a product or service, it's based on objective criteria. If an affiliate link is present, it is disclosed and never influences our opinion.

## Health disclaimer

Health-related content is provided **for information only** and **is not a substitute for a veterinary consultation**. In an emergency or if in doubt, contact your veterinarian or an animal poison control center.

## Corrections

Spotted an error or something imprecise? Report it via the [contact page](/contact/): we correct quickly and transparently.
