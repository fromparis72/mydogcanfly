## Who am I?

Hi! I'm **Camille Roussel**, founder of *My Dog Can Fly* and of its French sister site, [Le Chien Voyageur](https://lechienvoyageur.com/). For more than ten years I've been crisscrossing Europe and beyond with my dog — from the Brittany coast to Alpine trails, on night trains, long car rides and, yes, plenty of flights. I've made all the beginner mistakes: the hotel that turned us away at check-in, the heatstroke narrowly avoided at a highway rest stop, the forgotten travel bag…

This site grew out of those experiences, the good ones and the not-so-good ones: **passing on what I wish someone had told me**, always checked against official and veterinary sources.

## My approach

I test, I verify, I cross-check. Every guide combines **first-hand experience** with **reliable references** (veterinarians, government agencies, airlines and other carriers). When I don't know, I say so; when a topic touches on health, I point you to a professional.
